Pakistan's Historical Timeline

Ancient Civilizations

3300-1300 BCE

Indus Valley Civilization flourished in regions of modern-day Pakistan, including Harappa and Mohenjo-daro.

Mughal Empire

1526-1857 CE

Mughal rulers built magnificent monuments like Badshahi Mosque, Lahore Fort, and established cultural centers.

British Colonial Period

1857-1947 CE

British rule brought railways, administrative systems, and modern infrastructure to the region.

Modern Pakistan

1947-Present

Independence led to the creation of Pakistan, development of new cities like Islamabad, and cultural renaissance.
