Multan - City of Saints

Ancient city of Sufi shrines and rich Siraiki culture

Travel Punjab Sufi Heritage Siraiki Culture

by Real Pakistan ‚Ä¢ January 15, 2026

Multan, known as the "City of Saints," is one of Pakistan's oldest cities with a history spanning over 5,000 years. Famous for its Sufi shrines, vibrant Siraiki culture, and traditional crafts, Multan offers a spiritual and cultural journey through time.

Historical Background

Multan's history dates back to ancient times when it was known as Malli-istan. The city has been a center of trade and spirituality, attracting Sufi saints who established their shrines here.

The city flourished under various Muslim dynasties and became renowned for its Sufi traditions, earning the title "City of Saints" due to the numerous shrines of revered Sufi masters.

Culture & Traditions

Multan's rich Siraiki culture includes:

  • Language: Siraiki, Punjabi, Urdu
  • Spirituality: Sufi traditions and shrines
  • Crafts: Blue pottery, camel skin products
  • Music: Traditional Siraiki folk songs
  • Festivals: Urs celebrations at shrines
